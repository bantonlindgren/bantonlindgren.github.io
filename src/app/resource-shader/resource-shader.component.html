Health/ Resource shader

Here is a project I made in my spare time and that would later be implemented in our second game project at The Game Assembly.

I used Diablo 3 shaders for reference. The resource shader is inspired by the monk spirit bar.

I was inspired to make this shader because I was just starting to learn HLSL and I saw it as a fun challenge to see if I could make a 3D looking sphere on a 2D plane.

I made four different grayscale textures in Substance Designer that I packed into different channels of an RGBA texture so I only had to load one texture onto the graphics card.

Two of the texture channels are different noise textures that I used to create the flowing liquid in the globes. One channel contains the highlights on the globe surface. The last channel is just a black to white circular gradient to provide the shaded effect.

I set the health bar content to pan at an angle of -23 degrees (same as earths rotation). The resource bar I am panning straight up since it will regenerate rapidly over time in the game and this will give a clear visual indication of this.

In order to make the content look more 3D I squished it together the farther away from the center it is.
